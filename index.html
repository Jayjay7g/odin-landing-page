Welcome

Welcome to Coffee Point, where every cup tells a story. At Coffee Point, we believe in more than just brewing coffee; we believe in cultivating community, fostering connections, and creating moments of joy in every sip. From our carefully sourced beans to our expertly trained baristas, we strive for excellence in every aspect of our coffee journey. Whether you're seeking a cozy spot to unwind, a vibrant atmosphere to catch up with friends, or simply a quick pick-me-up on your way to work, Coffee Point welcomes you with open arms and a warm cup of your favorite brew.

Some of Our Specialties

Cappuccino

Three equla parts of steamed milk, foamed milk, and espresso.

Frappe

A cold espresso drink blended with sugar, milk, and other flavors into a thick drink, similar to a milkshake.

Latte

One part espresso with three parts steamed milk with froth on top.

Macchiato

Espresso with a dollop of steamed milk added in a 4:1 ratio.
